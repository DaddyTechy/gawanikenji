Our Journey

Our Beginning

Founded in 2020, Karinderia Express started as a small family business with a passion for authentic Filipino cuisine. Our journey began with a simple goal: to share the rich flavors and traditions of Filipino cooking with our community.

Today, we continue to serve our customers with the same dedication and love for Filipino food that inspired us from the start.

Our Kitchen

Our state-of-the-art kitchen is where magic happens. We combine traditional cooking methods with modern techniques to create the perfect balance of authentic flavors and contemporary convenience.

Every dish is prepared with care, using only the finest ingredients sourced from local suppliers.
